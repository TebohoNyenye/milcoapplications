About Us

MILCO, which stands for Made_In_Lesotho_Cooperative, is the proposed cooperative retail chain-store being created by and will be incubated under, the National University of Lesotho (NUL) Innovation Hub. Its primary aim is to trade on and sell products and services produced in Lesotho only. The following proposal for assistance is based on the relationship that NUL has with the Lesotho National Development Corporation (LNDC), formalised in a written MOU in which the two national institutions pledge to work together in the area of innovation for Lesotho’s development.
